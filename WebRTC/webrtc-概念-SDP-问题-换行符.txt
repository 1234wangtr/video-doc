A session is described by a series of fields, one per line

Each line is separated from the next by a carriage return/line feed sequence. 
Implementations are allowed to relax this to omit the carriage return and supply only the line feed.

解释：
carriage return 	是 \r 	简写 CR
line feed 			是 \n	简写 LF

carriage	英[ˈkærɪdʒ]
美[ˈkærɪdʒ]
n.	运费; 运输，输送; （旧时载客的） 四轮马车; 〈英〉火车客车车厢;

feed	英[fi:d]
美[fid]
vt.	喂养; 满足（欲望等）; 向…提供; 供…作食物;
vi.	吃，以…为食; 流入，注入，进入（如油流入机器）; [电子学] 馈入;
n.	饲料（尤指粗饲料），施肥; 喂送; 草料; （尤指向地方性电视台） 馈送电视节目;
