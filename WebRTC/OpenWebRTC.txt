A mobile-first WebRTC client framework for building native apps
With OpenWebRTC you can build native WebRTC apps 
that communicate with browsers that supports the WebRTC standard, 
such as Chrome, Firefox and Bowser. 

OpenWebRTC is especially focused on mobile platforms, 
with powerful features such as 
1）hardware accelerated video coding and 
2）OpenGL-based video rendering. 

The OpenWebRTC project is free and Open Source with a permissive BSD-2 license. 
The code is available on GitHub, 
https://github.com/EricssonResearch/OpenWebRTC
there is a mailing list and Twitter account for discussions and questions. 
This site and its blog are maintained by Ericsson Research.
